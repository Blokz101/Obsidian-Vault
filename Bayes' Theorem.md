- ## Definition
	- Allows you to reverse the order of the given [[Event|Events]]:
	  > [!Equation]
	  > For all [[Event|Events]] $A$ and $B$:
	  > $$\large{P(A|B)=\frac{P(B\mid A)*P(A)}{P(B)}}$$
	  > or
	  > $$\large{P(B)*P(A\mid B)=P(A) * P(B\mid A)}$$
