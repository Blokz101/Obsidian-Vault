- ## Definition
	- > [!Equation]
	  > If $f$ is [[Continuity|Continuous]] on $[a,b]$ then there exists some value $C$ in $[a,b]$ such that $f(C)$ equals the [[Average Value Of A Function]]:
	  > $$\large{f(C)=f_{avg}=\frac{1}{b-a}\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx}$$
