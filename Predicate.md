- ## Definition
	- A [[Proposition]] who's truth value is a function of one or more variables.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $x$ is an odd number
	- Notated in this format:
		- > [!Equation]
		  > $$\large{P(x):\text{x is a prime number}}$$
		  > or
		  > $$\large{P(5)=\text{True}}$$
	- A [[Predicate]] can be turned into a [[Proposition]] with a [[Quantifier]].
