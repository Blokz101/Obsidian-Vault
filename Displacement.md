- ## Definition
	- How far an object has moved from its starting position.
	- Symbol: $\Delta \vec{r}$
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\Delta \vec{r}=\vec{r}_{f}-\vec{r}_{i}}$$
	  > 
	  > $\Delta \vec{r}$ = [[Displacement]]
	  > $\vec{r}_{f}$ = Ending Position [[Vector]]
	  > $\vec{r}_{i}$ = Starting Position [[Vector]]
