- ## Definition
	- A unit that tells a materials ability to store an [[Charge]].
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{F=\frac{s^2c^2}{kgm}}$$
	  > 
	  > $F$ = [[Farad]]
	  > $s$ = Seconds
	  > $c$ = [[Coulomb]]
	  > $kg$ = Kilogram
	  > $m$ = Meter
