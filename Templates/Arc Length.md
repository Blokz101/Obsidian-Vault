## Definition
The length of an arc or curved line. For example, the length of $f(x)$ on the interval $[a,b]$. 

There are three versions
- Function of X
- Function of Y
- Parabolic

##### Function of X
> [!Equation]
> $$\text{Arc Length}=\int_{a}^{b} \sqrt{ 1+\left( \frac{dy}{dx} \right)^2 } \, dx$$
