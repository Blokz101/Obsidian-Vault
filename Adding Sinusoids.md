- ## Definition
	- Adding two sinusoids with the same [[Frequency]] and [[Phase Angle|Phase]]
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{a\cos(\alpha)+b\sin(\alpha)=\sqrt{ a^2 +b^2}\cos\left( \alpha-\arctan\left( \frac{b}{a} \right) \right)}$$
