- ## Definition
	- A 1 [[Bit]] [[Gated D-Latch|Register]].
	- Two inputs:
		- D (Data): Input data.
		- WE (Write Enable): Allow the data input to change the [[Gated D-Latch|Register]] output.
	- ![[IMG_E63C961AADD0-1.jpeg]]
	- They can also be put in series to store multiple [[Bit|Bits]].
