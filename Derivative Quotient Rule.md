- ## Definition
  - > [!Quotient Rule]
  > If $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ are both [[Derivative|Differentiable]] then:
  > $$\frac{d}{dx}[f/g](x)=\large{\frac{f(x)*\frac{d}{dx}g(x)-g(x)*\frac{d}{dx}f(x)}{g(x)^2}}$$
  > 
  > > [!Memory Aid]
