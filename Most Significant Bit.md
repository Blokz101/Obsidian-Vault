#Permanent [[Computer Systems]]

- ## Definition
	- The leftmost [[Bit]] in a [[Word]] or the [[Bit]] that represents the largest number.
	- Also the [[Bit]] that indicates the sign of the [[Two's Complement]] [[Signed Integer]].
