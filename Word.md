#Permanent [[Computer Systems]]

- ## Definition
	- A collection of [[Bit|Bits]].
	- [[Word|Word Length]] is how many bits are in the [[Word]].
	- Typical [[Word|Word Lengths]]
		- 4 [[Bit|Bits]]
			- Also called a [[Nybble]]
		- 8 [[Bit|Bits]]
			- Also called a [[Byte]]
		- 16 [[Bit|Bits]]
		- 32 [[Bit|Bits]]
		- 64 [[Bit|Bits]]
