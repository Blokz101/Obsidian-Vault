- ## [[Wavelength]]
	- You can find the [[Wavelength]] of the "string" with the following equation:
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\lambda=\frac{2L}{n}$$
	  > 
	  > $\lambda$ = [[Wavelength]] (cm)
	  > $L$ = Length of the string (cm)
	  > $n$ = Energy Level ([[Integer]])
