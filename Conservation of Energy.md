- ## Definition
	- The amount of [[Energy (Physics)]] in the universe is constant. Using this we can derive a few formulas.
	- > [!Equation]
	  >  $$\Delta E=W$$
	  >  $$\Delta K+\Delta U=W$$
	  >  $$U_{i}+K_{i}=U_{f}+K_{f}+W$$
