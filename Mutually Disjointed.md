- ## Definition
	- Two [[Set|Sets]] are [[Mutually Disjointed]] if:
	  > [!Equations]
	  > Given $n$ [[Set|Sets]] $(A_{1}, A_{2},\dots,A_{n})$
	  > $$\large{A_{i}\cap A_{j}=\emptyset \text{ for all }i\neq j}$$
