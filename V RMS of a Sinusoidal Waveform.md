- ## Definition
	- The [[Root Mean Square|RMS]] [[Voltage]] for a **Sinusoidal Waveform** with an [[Average Voltage]] ($V_{dc}$) of $0$ can be shortened into this :
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{V_{rms}=\frac{V_{p}}{\sqrt{ 2 }}}$$
	  > $V_{rms}$ = [[Root Mean Square|RMS]] [[Voltage]] in $V$
	  > $V_{p}$ = [[Amplitude]] in $V$
