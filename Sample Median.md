- ## Definition
	- The middle value of the values in [[Sample]] ordered from lowest to highest.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$
\large{
\tilde{x}=\left\{
\begin{array}
xx_{\left( \frac{n+1}{2} \right)}, & \text{if n is odd} \\
\frac{1}{2}\left( x_{\left( \frac{n}{2} \right)} + x_{\left( \frac{n}{2}+1 \right)} \right), & \text{ if n is even}
\end{array}
\right\}
}
$$
