# NaN
#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- The measure of the average [[Rate of Change]] of a [[Function]] over an [[Interval]].
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$Dq=\frac{f(x+h)+f(x)}{h}$$
	  > $Dq$ = [[Difference Quotient]] for $f(x)$
	  > $h$ = Length of [[Interval]]
