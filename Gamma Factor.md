- ## Definition
	- The factor at which [[Mass]] increases as [[Velocity]] approaches the [[Speed of Light]].
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\gamma=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1-\left( \frac{v}{c} \right)^2 }}}$$
	  > 
	  > $v$ = [[Velocity]]
	  > $c$ = [[Speed of Light]]
