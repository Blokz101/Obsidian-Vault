#Permanent [[Chemistry]]

- ## Definition
	- The change in **heat** at a **constant** [[Pressure]].
	> [!Equation]
	> $$\Delta E_{system}=q+w$$
	> 
	> $\Delta E_{system}$ = [[Energy]] of the system
	> $q$ = [[Enthalpy]]
	> $w$ = [[Work]]
