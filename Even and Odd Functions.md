- ## Definition
	- A function is **even** when it symmetric over the **y axis**. If it is not then it is **odd**.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > [[Even and Odd Functions|Even Function]]:
	  > $$f(-t)=f(t)$$
	  > 
	  > [[Even and Odd Functions|Odd Function]]:
	  > $$f(-t)=-f(t)$$
