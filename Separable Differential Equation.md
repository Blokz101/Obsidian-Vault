- ## Definition
	- A first **order** [[Differential Equation]] for which we can find a family of solutions.
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{f(x)}{g(y)}$$
	  > $$g(y)dy=f(x)dx$$
	  > $$\int g(y) \, dy=\int f(x) \, dx$$
