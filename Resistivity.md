- ## Definition
	- Property of a material independent from physical factors. How much a material opposes the flow of [[Electron|Electrons]] though it.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $\large{R=\rho\frac{L}{A}}$
	  > 
	  > $R$ = [[Resistivity]] in $\Omega$
	  > $L$ = Length in $m$
	  > $A$ = [[Area]] in $m^2$
	  > 
	  > 
	- Symbol is $\rho$
