- ## Definition
	- A simple switch that can be combined into complex logical systems.
	- MOS: Metal Oxide Semiconductor
	- There are two types:
		- N-Type
			- Closed by default.
			- Open with power.
			- ![[IMG_CB38D76063E8-1.jpeg|200]]
		- P-Type
			- Open by default.
			- Closed with power.
			- ![[IMG_CB38D76063E8-1.jpeg.jpeg|200]]
