- ## Definition
	- A function made of two [[Unit Step Function|Unit Step Functions]] in the following format:
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{p(t)=u(t)-u(t-t_{0})}$$
	  > 
	  > $u(t)$ = [[Unit Step Function]]
	  > $t_{0}$ = The time when the [[Unit Pulse Function]] returns to $0$
