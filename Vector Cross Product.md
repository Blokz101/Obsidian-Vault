- ## Definition
	- A way to multiply the **perpendicular** components of two [[Vector|Vectors]].
	- The exact opposite of the [[Vector Dot Product]].
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\lvert \vec{A}\times \vec{B} \rvert=\lvert \vec{A} \rvert\lvert \vec{B} \rvert\sin(\theta)}$$
