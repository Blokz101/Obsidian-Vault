- ## Definition
	- A method of specifying the [[Direction]] of the [[Vector]].
	- Symbol: $\large{\hat{v}}$
	- All [[Unit Vector]] are **less than one**.
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\hat{v}=\frac{\vec{v}}{|\vec{v}|}}$$
	  > 
	  > $\vec{v}$ = [[Vector]]
	- #### Conversion to Vector
		- > [!Equation]
		  > $$\large{\vec{v}=<\cos(|\vec{v}|*\theta_{x}),|\vec{v}|*\cos(\theta_{y}),|\vec{v}|*\cos(\theta_{x})>}$$
		  > 
		  > $|\vec{v}|*$
