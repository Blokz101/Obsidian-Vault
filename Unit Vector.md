- ## Definition
	- A method of specifying the [[Angular Direction]] of the [[Vector]].
	- Symbol: $\large{\hat{v}}$
	- All [[Unit Vector]] are **less than one**.
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\hat{v}=\frac{\vec{v}}{|\vec{v}|}}$$
	  > 
	  > $\vec{v}$ = [[Vector]]
