#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- The smallest [[Upper and Lower Bound|Upper Bound]] for a [[Set]].
	  > [!Example]
	  > {-1, 0, 1} has a [[Least Upper Bound]] of 1.
	- There can only be one [[Least Upper Bound]] per [[Set]].
