#Permanent [[Chemistry]]

- ## Definition
	- Quantitative measure of how much [[Solute]] vs [[Solvent]] is present in a [[Solution]].
	- It is measured in [[Molarity]] or the number of [[Mole|Moles]] of [[Solute]] present per liter of [[Solution]].
