- ## Definition
	- A [[Subset]] S [[In|âˆˆ]] $\mathbb{R}$ (S in [[Real Number|Real Numbers]]) is bounded above or below if there is a [[Real Number]] M such that M is greater/less than or equal to all [[Element|Elements]] in S.
