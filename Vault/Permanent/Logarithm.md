#Permanent [[Mathematics]]

- ## Definition
	- A [[Logarithm]] is the number($c$) with which you must raise the base($b$) to obtain a resulting number($a$) .
	> [!Equation]
	> $$\large{log_b(a)=c \leftarrow\rightarrow b^c=a}$$
- ## Rules
	- > [!Product Rule]
	  >$$log(xy)=log(x)+log(y)$$
