#Permanent [[Chemistry]]

- ## Definition
	- The number of **bonds** between two [[Atom|Atoms]]. A higher [[Bond Order]] means:
		- The closer the [[Atom|Atoms]] / Shorter the **bond**.
		- The stronger the **bond**.
		- The higher the **bond** energy.
