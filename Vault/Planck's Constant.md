#Permanent [[Chemistry]]

- ## Definition
	- A constant gives the relationship between the [[Energy]] of a photon and its [[Frequency]].
	  $$\large{6.626e-34Js}$$
	- This equation relates [[Energy]] to [[Frequency]].
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$E=h*v$$
	  > 
	  > $E$ = [[Energy]] (J)
	  > $h$ = [[Planck's Constant]] = $6.626e-34$ ($Js$).
	  > $v$ = [[Frequency]] ($s^{-1}$ ). ^103390
