#Permanent [[Chemistry]]

- ## Definition
	- f
- ## Absolute Zero
	- The [[Temperature]] at which any amount of a gas has a [[Volume]] of **zero (or very close to it)**.
	- Also $-273.15$ in [[Temperature|C]].
- ## Units
	- Celsius (c)
	- Fahrenheit (f)
		- $f=(c*\frac{9}{5})+32$
		- $c=(f-32)*\frac{5}{9}$
	- Kelvin (k)
		- $k=c+273.15$
		- $c=k-273.15$
