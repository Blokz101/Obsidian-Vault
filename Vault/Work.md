- ## Definition (Physics)
	- Measure of [[Energy]] transfer that occurs when an object is moved over a distance by an external force.
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$W=Fd$$
	  > $W$ = [[Work]]
	  > $F$ = [[Force]]
	  > $d$ = [[Distance]]
- ## Definition (Chemistry)
	- The organized movement of [[Molecule|Molecules]].
