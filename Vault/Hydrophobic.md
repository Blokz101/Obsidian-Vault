#Permanent [[Chemistry]]

- ## Definition
	- Repeals water.
	- Term used to describe a [[Compound]] with [[Polarity|Non-Polar]] regions that have weak to no interactions with water.
- ## Reference
	- Opposite of [[Hydrophilic]].
