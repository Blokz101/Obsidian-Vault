#Permanent [[Electricity]]

- Definition
	- The rate at witch [[Work]] is done
	- Basically [[Acceleration]], but for [[Work]] instead of [[Position]].
	- General [[Power]] equation
	  >$$P=\frac{W}{t}$$
	  > $P$ = [[Power]]
	  > $W$ = [[Work]]
	  > $t$ = Time
- Symbol
	- Generally $P$
