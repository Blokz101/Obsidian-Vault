# NaN
#Permanent [[Calculus I]]

- ## Definition
	- The largest [[Upper and Lower Bound|Lower Bound]] for a [[Set]].
	  > [!Example]
	  > {-1, 0, 1} has a [[Greatest Lower Bound]] of -1.
	- There can only be one [[Greatest Lower Bound]] per [[Set]].
- ## Reference
	- Opposite of [[Least Upper Bound]].
