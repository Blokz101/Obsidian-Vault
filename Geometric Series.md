- ## Definition
	- An [[Infinite Series]] that takes the form of:
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\sum^{\infty}_{n=0}ar^{n}}$$
	  > $$a+ar+ar^2+ar^3+ar^4+\dots+ar^n$$
	- We can determine **convergence** with these rules:
	- > [!Equation]
	  > Case|Status|Sum
	  > ---|---|---
	  > $1$|Diverges|NA
	  > $\mid r\mid<1$|Converges|$S=\frac{a}{1-r}$
