- ## Definition
	- Symbol: $\vec{p}$
	- For speeds less then 10% the [[Speed of Light]], this equation is very accurate:
		- > [!Equation]
		  > $$\large{\vec{p}\approx m\vec{v}}$$
		  > 
		  > $m$ = [[Mass]]
		  > $\vec{v}$ = [[Velocity]]
	- For speeds above 10% the [[Speed of Light]], you must consider that as you approach the [[Speed of Light]] ---
