- ## Definition
	- > [!Equation]
	  > The [[Differentiable of a Function]] at $(a,b)$ is:
	  > $$df=f_{x}(a,b)(x-a)+f_{y}(a,b)(y-b)$$
	  > $$df=f_{x}(a,b)\Delta x+f_{y}(a,b)\Delta y$$
	  > $$df=f_{x}(a,b)dx+f_{y}(a,b)dy$$
