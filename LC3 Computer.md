- ## Definition
	- Stands for Little Computer 3 and is used for teaching assembly etc.
	- Contains 7 registers with the labels `R0` to `R6`. 
	- Register purposes:
		- `R0`: General Purpose.
		- `R1`: General Purpose.
		- `R2`: General Purpose.
		- `R3`: General Purpose.
		- `R4`: General Purpose.
		- `R5`: General Purpose.
		- `R6`: General Purpose.
		- `R7`: Used for [[LC3 Return]] addresses.
