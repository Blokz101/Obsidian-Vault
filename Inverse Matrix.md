- ## Definition
	- The [[Matrix]] $B$ is called the inverse of [[Matrix]] $A$ if:
		- > [!Equation]
		  > $$\large{AB = BA=I}$$
		  > 
		  > $A$ and $B$ = Arbitrary [[Square Matrix]]
		  > $I$ = [[Identity Matrix]]
	- For a $2\times2$ [[Matrix]] $A$this formula can simply be applied.
