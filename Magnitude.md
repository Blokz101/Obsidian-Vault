- ## Definition
	- The length of a [[Vector]].
		- [[Magnitude]] does not have [[Direction]].

		  > [[Magnitude]] Will 
	- Symbol: $\large{|\vec{v}|}$
	- We can use [[Pathergrans Theorem]] to find the [[Magnitude]].
		- > [!Equation]
		  > $$\large{|\vec{v}|}=\sqrt{ x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} }$$
