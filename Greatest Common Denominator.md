- ## Definition
	- The greatest number that divides two other numbers.
	- Two numbers that are co prime will have a [[Greatest Common Denominator|GCD]] of $1$.
		- See [[Mathematical Numerical Definitions]].
	- Notated as $gcd(x,y)$.
