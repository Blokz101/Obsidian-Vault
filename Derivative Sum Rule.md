#Permanent [[Calculus I]]

- ## Definition
  - > [!Sum Rule]
  > If $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ are both [[Derivative|Differentiable]] then:
  > $$\frac{d}{dx}[f+g](x)=\frac{d}{dx}f(x)+\frac{d}{dx}g(x)$$
