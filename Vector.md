- ## Definition
	- [[Vector|Vectors]] are a description of [[Position]] or [[Velocity]]
	- ![[Drawing 2023-01-13 17.27.05.excalidraw]]
	- They are notated with a little arrow above the character or as a series of variables:
		- $\large{\vec{v}}$
		- $<x,y,z>$
	- [[Vector|Vectors]] have:
		- [[Position]]
		- [[Displacement]]
		- [[Magnitude]]
