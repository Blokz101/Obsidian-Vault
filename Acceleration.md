- ## Definition
	- The [[Derivative]] of [[Velocity]]
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\vec{a}_{avg}=\frac{\Delta \vec{v}}{\Delta t}=\frac{\vec{r}_{f}-\vec{r}_{i}}{t_{f}-t_{i}}}$$
	  > 
	  > $\vec{r}_{f}$ = Final [[Velocity]] [[Vector]]
	  > $\vec{r}_{i}$ = Initial [[Velocity]] [[Vector]]
	  > $t_{f}$ = Final [[Time]]
	  > $t_{i}$ = Initial [[Time]]
