#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- A function in witch $x$ and $y$ are functions of $\theta$. Note that $\theta$ can be any parameter.
	  > [!Example]
	  > For example $x$ and $y$ might be functions of time:
	  > $x=f(t)$
	  > $y=f(t)$
