- ## Definition
	- [[Current]] is how much [[Charge]] moves per unit of time.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $\large{I=\frac{dQ}{dt}}$
	  > 
	  > $I$ = [[Current]] in $A$
	  > $Q$ = [[Charge]] in $C$
	  > $t$ = Time in $s$
	- Symbol is $I$

Current is related to [[Voltage]] with [[Ohm's Law]].
