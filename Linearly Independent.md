- ## Definition
	- Two functions are **Linearly Dependent** on each other if they can equal each other by multiplied by some constant $C$:
		- > [!Equation]
		  > **Linearly Dependent** if:
		  > $$\large{f(x)=Cg(x)}$$
		  > 
		  > Where $C$ is constant
	- The two functions are **Linearly independent** for all other cases.
