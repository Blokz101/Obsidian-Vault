- ## Definition
	- Simply [[Decibel]] but with reference to a [[Power]] of $1\,dB$. Basically [[Decibel]] but describing [[Power]].
	- Notated as $dBW$.
	- To convert from $W$ to $dBW$:
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{P_{[dBW]}=10\log_{10}(P_{[dB]})}$$
