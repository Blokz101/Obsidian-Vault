- ## Definition
	- The average of the values in the [[Sample]].
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\bar{x}=\frac{1}{n}\sum^{}_{i}x_{i}}$$
	- The [[Sample Mean]] is not robust against [[Outliers]], one extreme value can throw it of.
