- ## Definition
	- > [!Equation]
	  Let $A$, $B$, and $C$ be [[Set|Sets]]
	  > $$\large{A\cap (B\cup C)=(A\cap B)\cup(A\cap C)}$$
	  > $$\large{A\cup(B\cap C)=(A\cup B)\cap(A\cup C)}$$
