- ## Definition
	- In order to integrate $Sin$ or $Cos$ that are raised to a high **odd** power we can use this this formula to expand them:
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $\large{Sin^2(x)=\frac{1}{2}(1-Cos(2x))}$
	  > $\large{Cos^2(x)=\frac{1}{2}(1+Cos(2x))}$
