# NaN
#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- An equation that relates the sides of a triangle together and an angle.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{c=\sqrt{a^2+b^2-2ab*\cos(C)}}$$
	  > $$\large{C=\arccos(\frac{a^2+b^{2}-c^{^2}}{2ab})}$$
	  > $c$ = Length of side $c$
	  > $a$ = Length of side $a$
	  > $b$ = Length of side $b$
	  > $C$ = Angle $C$
- ## Reference
	- Similar to the [[Law of Sines]]
