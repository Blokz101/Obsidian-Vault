#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- Refers to an **infinitesimally small change** in some varying quantity.
		- For example an **infinitesimally small change** in **$x$** is **$dx$**.
	- The [[Derivative]] is always the ratio of [[Differentials]] $\frac{dy}{dx}$.
	- $df=f'(x)dx$
