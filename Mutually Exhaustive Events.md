- ## Definition
	- Two [[Event|Events]] $A$ and $B$ are [[Mutually Exhaustive Events]] if $A\cup B=\Omega$.
		- Basically if the entire [[Sample Space]] is covered by $A$, $B$, or both, then the [[Event|Events]] are [[Mutually Exhaustive Events]].
