- ## Definition
	- A method of taking [[Derivative|Derivatives]] of functions inside functions.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > Let $f(y)=z$ and $g(x)=y$:
	  > $$\large{\frac{d}{dx}z}=\frac{d}{dy}f(y)* \frac{d}{dx}g(x)$$
	  > Note that $z=f(g(x))$ because of substitution.
