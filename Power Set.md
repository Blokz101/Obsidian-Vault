- ## Definition
	- Let $A$ be a [[Set]] and $P(A)$ be the [[Power Set]] of $A$ **including** the [[Empty Set]].
	- A [[Set]] of all [[Subset|Subsets]] of $A$.
	- Its [[Cardinality]] is $2^x$ where $x$ is the [[Cardinality]] of $A$.
