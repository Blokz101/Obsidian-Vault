- ## Definition
	- Distance from [[Average Voltage]] to either max or min peak.
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{A=\frac{V_{H}-V_{L}}{2}}$$
	  > 
	  > $A$ = [[Amplitude]] in $V$
	  > $V_{H}$ = Max [[Voltage]] in $V$
	  > $V_{L}$ = Min [[Voltage]] in $V$
