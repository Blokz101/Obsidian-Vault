#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- A number where two conflicting rules apply.
	- > [!Example]
	  > Most common types:
	  > 
	  >  | 
	  > ---|---
	  > $0*\infty$|$\frac{0}{0}$
	  > $\infty-\infty$|$0^0$
	  > $\frac{\infty}{\infty}$|$1^\infty$
- ## Reference
	- Useful in [[L'Hopital's Rule]].
