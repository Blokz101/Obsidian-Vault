- ## Definition
	- Because the change in [[Energy (Physics)]] in the universe is always 0, when [[Work (Physics)]] is done on a system, the [[Energy (Physics)]] has to come from somewhere. It comes from [[Potential Energy]].
	- > [!Equation]
	  > $$\large{\Delta K=W}$$
	  > $$\large{\Delta U=-W}$$
