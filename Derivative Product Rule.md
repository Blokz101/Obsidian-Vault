#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
  - > [!Product Rule]
  > If $f(x)$ and $g(x)$ are both [[Derivative|Differentiable]] then:
  > $$\frac{d}{dx}[f*g](x)=f(x)*\frac{d}{dx}g(x)+g(x)*\frac{d}{dx}f(x)$$
