#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- The [[Set]] of all [[Rational Numbers]] and [[Irrational Numbers]].
	  > [!Example]
	  > ℝ = {x | -∞ < x < ∞}
	  > or
	  > (-∞, ∞)
	- Notated with the symbol ℝ
