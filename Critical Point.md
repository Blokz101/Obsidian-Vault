#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- The point at which a [[Function]] switches from **increasing to decreasing** or from **decreasing to increasing**.
	- Can be found where:
		- The first [[Derivative]] of a function is equal to 0.
		- The first [[Derivative]] of a function does not exist.
