- ## Definition
	- A way of representing [[Resistance]] in the [[Phasor Domain]].
	- Measured in $\Omega$.
	- [[Resistor|Resistors]] are completely real. So a [[Resistor]] with a resistance of $4\Omega$ will have an [[Impedance]] of $4\Omega$.
