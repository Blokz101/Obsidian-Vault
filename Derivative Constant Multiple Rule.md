#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
  - > [!Constant Multiple Rule]
  > If $f(x)$ is [[Derivative|Differentiable]] and $K$ is **constant** then:
  > $$\frac{d}{dx}Kf(x)=K*\frac{d}{dx}f(x)$$
