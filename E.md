#Permanent [[Calculus]]

- ## Definition
	- A special number such that:
	  > [!Equation]
	  > $$^{lim}_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{e^h-1}{h}=1$$
- ## [[Derivative]] of [[E]]
	> [!E Rule]
	> $$\frac{d}{dx}e^x=e^x$$
	> > [!Proof]-
	> > Step|Explanation
	> > ---|---
	> > $\frac{d}{dx}e^x=^{lim}_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{e^{h+x}-e^x}{h}$|Given
	> > $\frac{d}{dx}e^x=^{lim}_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{e^{h}e^{x}-e^x}{h}$
	> >
